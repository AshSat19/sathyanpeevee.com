About Me

When I started off my life as a photographer and videographer more than three decades ago, the beginnings were humble, in a small town in the southern Indian state of Kerala. Soon after, the migration to the Sultanante of Oman happened. Ever since then, the journey has been a learning experience that continues to this day.

'Quality over Quantity' is the mantra I have always tried to work by because at the end of the day, completing and delivering one good work prevails over delivering ten mediocre ones. From the age of the Polaroid and the Beta Max, the SLRs and the VHS, all the way down to where we stand right now - mirrorless DSLR and 4K video, keeping up with the trend and striving to deliver work of industry standards. Opportunities to work with an esteemed clientele and the ability to cater to their needs and expecattions is the reward I value above all, as has been the case till date.
